## Official Search Priority
1. Official conference proceedings or program pages
2. DBLP metadata pages
3. Official lab, company, or project paper pages
4. arXiv abstract pages for high-impact preprints
5. Google Scholar only for citation lookups when needed

## Exclusions
- Workshops unless the paper is already clearly field-defining
- Unreviewed blog posts
- Low-citation arXiv uploads with unclear adoption
